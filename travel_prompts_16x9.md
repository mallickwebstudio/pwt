# Custom AI Image Prompts for Travel Packages

> **Note:** All prompts are optimized for a 16:9 aspect ratio and enforce strict 'no text' rules. Generating realistic composites (all features in one frame) is notoriously difficult for AI without creating a messy collage. Therefore, the 'Composite/Poster' prompts below use a cinematic double-exposure/montage style to force the AI to blend the environments beautifully rather than awkwardly placing them next to each other.
